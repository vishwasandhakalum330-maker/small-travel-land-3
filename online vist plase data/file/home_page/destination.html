Sigiriya Rock Fortress (Lion Rock)–Central Province

An ancient rock citadel built by King Kashyapa in the 5th century. Famous for its frescoes, mirror wall, and the giant lion’s paw entrance. UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic landmarks.

Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) – Kandy

Location: Kandy City. Highlights: Sacred relic of Lord Buddha’s tooth. History: A UNESCO site, central to Sri Lankan Buddhism. Activities: Witness Kandy Esala Perahera festival, temple rituals, museum visit. Best Time: July – August (Perahera season), or December – April.

Arugam Bay – Eastern Province

Location: Ampara District. Highlights: Surfer’s paradise, laid-back beaches. Activities: Surfing, lagoon tours, visit Kumana National Park. Best Time: April – October (surfing season).

Yala National Park – Southern Province

Location: Hambantota & Monaragala Districts. Highlights: Highest leopard density in the world. History: Established as a wildlife sanctuary in 1900, national park in 1938. Activities: Safari tours to see elephants, leopards, sloth bears, and birds. Best Time: February – July (dry season, best wildlife viewing).

Anuradhapura – North Central Province

Location: Anuradhapura District. Highlights: Jetavanaramaya stupa, Ruwanwelisaya, Sri Maha Bodhi. History: First ancient capital (4th century BC – 11th century AD). Activities: Visit ancient ruins, pilgrimage to Bodhi tree, explore reservoirs. Best Time: May – September.

Adams Peak (Sri Pada) – Central Province

Location: Nuwara Eliya District. Highlights: Sacred footprint at summit. History: Revered by Buddhists, Hindus, Christians, and Muslims. Activities: Pilgrimage hike, sunrise view from the peak. Best Time: December – May (climbing season).

Galle Fort – Southern Province

Location: Galle City. Highlights: Dutch and Portuguese colonial fort, cobblestone streets. History: Built in 1588 by the Portuguese, expanded by the Dutch. Activities: Walk along ramparts, visit cafes, explore museums and boutiques. Best Time: December – April.

Ella – Uva Province

Location: Badulla District. Highlights: Nine Arches Bridge, Ravana Falls, Ella Rock. History: Colonial-era railway town, now a backpacker hub. Activities: Hiking, photography, train rides through tea plantations. Best Time: March – June, December – February.
